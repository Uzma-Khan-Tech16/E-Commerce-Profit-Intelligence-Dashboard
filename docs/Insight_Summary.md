# 📊 E-Commerce Profit Intelligence Dashboard

## Business Insight Summary

## Project Overview

The objective of this project was to analyze the Global Superstore dataset and transform raw business data into meaningful insights using Python, MySQL, and Power BI.

The dashboard focuses on sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional market analysis to support data-driven business decisions.

## 2. Sales Analysis

Purpose:
Analyze revenue generation and customer purchasing trends.

Key Findings:

- Technology generated the highest sales.
- Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest sales volume.
- Western Europe recorded the highest regional sales.
- Several customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.


## 3. Profit Analysis

Purpose:
Evaluate profitability across different business dimensions.

Key Findings:

- Asia Pacific generated the highest profit.
- Technology category was the most profitable.
- Higher discounts generally resulted in lower profitability.
- A small number of products generated most of the profit.
- Loss-making products were identified for further review.


## 4. Customer & Market Insights

Purpose:
Understand customer behavior and market performance.

Key Findings:

- Consumer segment represented the largest customer base.
- Asia Pacific remained the strongest-performing market.
- Technology products consistently outperformed other categories.
- Sales were concentrated among a limited number of high-value customers.
- Regional performance varied significantly across markets.


# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

- Increase investment in high-performing Technology products.
- Improve profitability by reviewing high-discount products.
- Focus marketing efforts on profitable customer segments.
- Expand business operations in high-performing markets such as Asia Pacific.
- Review low-performing products and optimize inventory.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies for top customers.
